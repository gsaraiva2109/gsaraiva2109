## About Me.

<div align="justify">
  
I'm an **Infrastructure Architect** obsessed with optimization and structure. I leverage **AI-Accelerated Engineering** not to cut corners, but to bypass syntax drudgery so I can focus entirely on **System Design and Logic**. For me, code is a tool, but Architecture is the product.

When it comes to **Virtualization** and **Network Security**, I build for production. I operate a dedicated **Proxmox VE** environment (The "K6 Node") where I manage strict **Zero-Trust** policies via **Cloudflare** and **OPNsense**, and orchestrate containerized fleets using **Kubernetes** and **Docker**.

I am a true believer in the **"Terminal First"** philosophy. As an **Arch Linux** daily driver, I am comfortable living in the CLI, managing systems via SSH, and writing Bash scripts to automate the mundane. I don't just use tools; I configure them to fit my workflow perfectly.

Beyond the tech stack, **Homelabbing** is my craft. Whether I'm building middleware to bridge legacy ERPs, designing **n8n** automation flows, or refining my ZFS storage pools, I do it because I genuinely enjoy the complexity. I am the person you want when you need **solutions, reliability, and robust documentation.**
